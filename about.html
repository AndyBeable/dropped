About Us

The Story

Founded by Simon Mottram in London in 2004, Dropped is the world’s one-stop shop for cycling news, events, gear, routes. For 15 years, we've products have redefined comfort, performance, and style for cyclists from absolute beginners through to WorldTour professionals. Born from a love of the sport, Dropped regularly organises and sponsors unique rides and events. The Dropped Cycling Club was founded in 2015 to bring cyclists together, and today there are already more than 17,000 members worldwide. Our 21 Clubhouses across Europe, North America and Asia have become hubs for DCC members and local cyclists, offering a café space and stocking the latest products.

The Misson

Dropped is proud to have worked with the sport's best athletes from its earliest days. First, as title sponsor to the British UCI Continental team Dropped from 2005 to 2012, then as clothing supplier to Team Sky from 2012 to 2016. We continue to work at the top tiers of professional cycling with the women’s UCI WorldTour team CANYON//SRAM since 2016, and, after publishing a two-year study on the state of the sport titled the Dropped Roadmap, we have returned to the men’s WorldTour with EF Education First Pro Cycling from 2019.
